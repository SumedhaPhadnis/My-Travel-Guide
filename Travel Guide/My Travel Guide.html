KOLKATA - THE CITY OF JOY


Kolkata is known for it's litrary, artistic and revolutionary heritage, as the former capital of India, it was the birthplace of modern Indian litrary and artistic thought . Kolkata is called the "Cultural capital of India". Take time to explore and you'll find dramatic colonial architecture and historic temple, art centers and museums and botanical gardens. Kolkata sprawls along both banks of the wide Hoogly River, but most of interest to travellers are on the east bank, between the two bridges.

FACTS
