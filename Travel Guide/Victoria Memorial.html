VICTORIA MEMORIAL

Victoria Memorial is one of the famous and beautiful monuments of Kolkata. This huge white-marble museum, made from Makrana marbles from Rajasthan, is filled with a vast collection of remnants from the period of British Empire rule in India. It was built between 1906 and 1921 to commemorate Queen Victoria's 25-year reign in India.

Location:The memorial lies on the Maidan, near Jawaharlal Nehru Road (also known as Chowringhee Road).
